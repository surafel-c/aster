The Hadiya Zone is located in the Central Ethiopia Regional State, approximately 230 kilometers southwest of Addis Ababa. Geographically situated in the western Ethiopian highlands, it serves as a vital agricultural hub. Its administrative center, Hosanna, sits at a high elevation, enjoying a temperate climate ideal for diverse crop production.
