Our Services

The department supports these thousands of households through a network of Development Agents (DAs) stationed at the kebele (village) level to provide direct training and resources.

Crop Advisory

Expert guidance on crop selection, planting schedules, and best practices for optimal yields.

Training & Education

Workshops, seminars, and courses to enhance farming skills and knowledge of modern techniques.

Farmer Cooperatives

Support for forming and managing cooperative groups to improve market access and bargaining power.

Market Information

Real-time market prices, demand forecasts, and buyer connections for better sales opportunities.

Permit & Licensing

Assistance with agricultural permits, certifications, and regulatory compliance.

Extension Services

On-site support from agricultural extension officers for personalized farm management.
